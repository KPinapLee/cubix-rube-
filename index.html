Rubik's Cube Notation Guide

Face Rotations:

F - Front clockwise | F' - Front counter-clockwise

B - Back clockwise | B' - Back counter-clockwise

U - Up clockwise | U' - Up counter-clockwise

D - Down clockwise | D' - Down counter-clockwise

R - Right clockwise | R' - Right counter-clockwise

L - Left clockwise | L' - Left counter-clockwise

Cube Rotations:

x - Rotate entire cube on R | x' - Rotate opposite

y - Rotate entire cube on U | y' - Rotate opposite

z - Rotate entire cube on F | z' - Rotate opposite

Note: Use lowercase letters for clockwise rotations, uppercase for counter-clockwise (e.g., 'r' for right clockwise, 'R' for right counter-clockwise)
